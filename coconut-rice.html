Coconut Rice

Description

Coconut rice is a dish made by cooking rice with coconut milk and water, and often some additional ingredients to add flavor and texture. This dish is popular in many parts of the world.

Coconut rice has a slightly sweet and creamy flavor that is distinct from regular rice. The addition of coconut milk not only adds a unique flavor to the rice, but also provides extra nutrients, such as healthy fats and vitamins.

This dish can be made with white rice, brown rice, or even black rice, depending on personal preference. Overall, coconut rice is a delicious and versatile dish that can be enjoyed as part of many different cuisines.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of rice
  • 1 can (15 ounce) of coconut milk
  • 1 (29 ounce) can diced tomatoes
  • 4 medium habanero peppers
  • 1 small green pepper
  • 1 small onions
  • 1/4 cup of vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt to taste
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • water

Steps

  1. Boil rice in water for 10-15 minutes (let it not be too soft, because it will be cooked more)
  2. Heat vegetable oil in a pot
  3. Add onions and stir with black pepper for one minute
  4. Add diced tomatoes, habaneros and coconut milk with salt. cover to cook till the mixture boils
  5. Add the rice and stir and cover till the rice is almost dry
  6. Add green peppers and let it simmer until rice absorbs its juice
  7. Enjoy

    8. note: feel free to add pepper and salt to your taste
